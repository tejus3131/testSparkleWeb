SparkleWeb


  • SparkleWeb is a simple, lightweight, and fast web framework for Python.

  • It is designed to be easy to use and easy to learn.

  • SparkleWeb is built on top of Flask and Bootstrap.

  • SparkleWeb is open source and free to use.

  • SparkleWeb is licensed under the MIT license.

  • SparkleWeb is developed by Tejus Gupta
